Mission

Enhance the circular battery economy by providing health transparency on battery life cycle.

Goals

Expand battery lifecycle knowledge

Thanks to our big data platform combining physics, chemistry and usage conditions.

Predict end of battery life

For each battery in a fleet.

Reduce the overall cost of your fleet

By optimizing the usage and replacing batteries at the right moment.

Simplify warranty and qualify second life

Thanks to our knowledge about the usage conditions of each battery.
